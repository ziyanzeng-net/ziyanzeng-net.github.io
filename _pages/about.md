I am a first-year PhD Student of Complex Systems and Networks, at IMT School for Advanced Studies Lucca, Italy. Currently, I focus on complex networks, evolutionary dynamics, network renormalization, and mathematical epidemiology. I am interested in discovering the patterns of stochastic dynamic processes and phase transitions in complex networks using both mathematical tools and computer simulations.
